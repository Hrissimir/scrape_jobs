Automation QA Engineer (Virtualization Backup)

Acronis Sofia, BG

1 week ago

Apply on company website
Virtualization backup team is responsible for integration of the company flagship product (Acronis Backup) with various virtualization platforms, in order to perform backup and restore operations with virtual machines (and hypervisor hosts).
In Virtualization backup team, you will be working on various challenging issues from various fields, such as:
  • Figuring out in-depth work of VMware ESXi, Hyper-V, Virtuozzo and other hypervisors to address platform-specific issues
  • Deep understanding of file systems management and parsing (FAT, NTFS, Ext, XFS, ReFS)
  • Understanding the boot sequence of operating systems to ensure bootability across different virtualization platforms during VM backup and restore
  • Performing integration with Azure, Google, Oracle, Amazon clouds, XenServer platforms

Virtualization team works both on implementation of brand new features and fixing existing product-related issues.

Responsibilities
  • Participate in building of automated testing framework
  • Design, code and execute automated test scenarios
  • Maintain and design test environments required for tests execution and manual tests
  • Work with other teams to understand the business requirements and logic, relevant use cases, scenarios and functional flows for the system under test
  • Participate in manual testing of the product features
SKILLS & EXPERIENCE:
  • Experience in manual QA of client-server applications and web-based systems (not less than 5 years)
  • Experience in tests automation development (not less than 3 years)
  • Experience with Python (not less than 3 years)
  • Prior experience with any virtualization platforms (VMware, Microsoft Hyperv, KVM)


Major Advantages
  • Prior experience with Cloud platforms and services
  • Experience with backup software
  • Experience with behavior-driven development (BDD)
